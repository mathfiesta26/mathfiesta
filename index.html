About the College

Kongu Engineering College (KEC), established in 1983 and affiliated with Anna University, Chennai, is a premier autonomous institution recognized for excellence in technical education and research. It offers interdisciplinary, value-based education that integrates theory and practice in a supportive academic environment.

About the Club

About the Event

Math Fiesta '26, orchestrated by the Srinivasa Ramanujan Mathematics Club at Kongu Engineering College, Perundurai, unfolds as a dynamic national-level symposium celebrating the power and beauty of mathematics. Step into a realm where every equation sparks curiosity and every moment opens the door to something extraordinary.
